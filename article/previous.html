ICST 2024

This year, four symposia will be held, Computer, Electronics, and Information Technology symposium; Life Sciences, Materials, and Applied Chemistry symposium; Mechanical and Industrial Engineering symposium; and Remote Sensing and Geomatics symposium. ICST UGM 2024 is in conjunction with the 5th Geoscience and Environmental Management symposium and the 5th International Conference on Smart and Innovative Agriculture.

The ICST 2024 has received over 385 submissions from 17 countries. The accepted and presented papers have been submitted for possible inclusion to journals/proceedings that are indexed by Scopus/CPCI/DOAJ: E3S Web of Conferences, AIP Conference Proceedings, Engineering Headway by Scientific.Net, Advances in Engineering Research by Atlantis Press.

 

ICST 2023

This year, five symposia has been held: Computer and Information Technology symposium; Electronics, Power, Communication, Control, and Instrumentation symposium; Life Sciences, Materials, and Applied Chemistry symposium; Mechanical and Industrial Engineering symposium; and Remote Sensing and Geomatics symposium. ICST 2023 is in conjunction with the 4th Geoscience and Environmental Management symposium.

The ICST 2023 has received over 289 submissions from 10 countries from the Netherlands, Pakistan, United Arab Emirates, India, Tunisia, Japan, Vietnam, Malaysia, Korea, and Indonesia. The accepted and presented papers have been submitted for possible inclusion to journals/proceedings that are indexed by Scopus/CPCI/DOAJ: E3S Web of Conferences.

ICST 2022

This year, five symposia will be held: Computer and Information Technology symposium; Electronics, Power, Communication, Control, and Instrumentation symposium; Life Sciences, Materials, and Applied Chemistry symposium; Mechanical and Industrial Engineering symposium; and Remote Sensing and Geomatics symposium. ICST 2022 is in conjunction with the 3rd Geoscience and Environmental Management symposium.

The ICST 2022 has received over 360 submissions from 10 countries throughout the world: Netherlands, Pakistan, United Arab Emirates, India, Tunisia, Japan, Vietnam, Malaysia, Korea, and Indonesia. The accepted and presented papers have been submitted for possible inclusion to journals/proceedings that are indexed by Scopus/CPCI/DOAJ: IEEE Xplore Digital Library, IOP Conference Series: Earth and Environmental Science, AIP Conference Proceedings, or Scientific.Net.

 

ICST 2021

This year, five symposia have been held, Computer and Information Technology symposium; Electronics, Power, Communication, Control, and Instrumentation symposium; Life Sciences, Materials, and Applied Chemistry symposium; Mechanical and Industrial Engineering symposium; and Remote Sensing and Geomatics symposium. ICST 2020 is in conjunction with the 2nd Geoscience and Environmental Management symposium.

The ICST 2021 has received over 290 submissions from 13 countries throughout the world: Indonesia, Malaysia, the Philippines, India, Taiwan, Japan, Iran, France, United Kingdom, Russia, Saudi Arabia, the USA, and Mexico. The accepted and presented papers have been published in the AIP Conference Proceeding, Key Engineering Materials by Scientific.Net, Materials Science Forum by Scientific.Net, and E3S Web of Conferences.

 

ICST 2020

This year, five symposia have been held, Computer and Information Technology symposium; Electronics, Power, Communication, Control, and Instrumentation symposium; Life Sciences, Materials, and Applied Chemistry symposium; Mechanical and Industrial Engineering symposium; and Remote Sensing and Geomatics symposium. ICST 2020 is in conjunction with the 1st Geoscience and Environmental Management symposium.

The ICST 2020 has received over 420 submissions from 20 countries throughout the world: Indonesia, the USA, India, Philippines, South Africa, Singapore, Russia, United Kingdom, Japan, Egypt, Myanmar, Mexico, Qatar, Australia, France, China, and others.
