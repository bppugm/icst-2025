ICST 2025

30–31 July 2025 in R. Soegondo Building, UGM Faculty of Cultural Sciences, Yogyakarta, Indonesia

This year, five symposia will be held, the 2nd Computer, Electronics, and Information Technology symposium; the 6th Geoscience and Environmental Management symposium; the 7th Life Sciences, Materials, and Applied Chemistry symposium; the 8th Mechanical and Industrial Engineering symposium; and the 8th Remote Sensing and Geomatics symposium. ICST UGM 2025 is in conjunction with the 6th International Conference on Bioinformatics, Biotechnology, and Biomedical Engineering and the 6th International Conference on Smart and Innovative Agriculture.

The ICST 2025 has received over 391 submissions from 19 countries. The accepted and presented papers will be submitted for possible inclusion to journals/proceedings that are indexed by Scopus/CPCI/DOAJ: Atlantis Highlights in Engineering by Atlantis Press, Atlantis Highlights in Sustainable Development by Atlantis Press, Indonesian Journal of Chemistry, Journal of Multidisciplinary Applied Natural Science.
