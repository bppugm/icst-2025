Held as part of the Universitas Gadjah Mada Annual Scientific Conferences (UASC) 2025 series, the 11th International Conference on Science and Technology (ICST UGM 2025) provides an ideal academic platform for researchers to present the latest research findings and describe emerging technologies and directions in engineering and the natural sciences.

This year, three symposia will be held, Life Sciences, Materials, and Applied Chemistry symposium; Mechanical and Industrial Engineering symposium; and Remote Sensing and Geomatics symposium.

ICST UGM 2025 is in conjunction with the Computer, Electronics, and Information Technology symposium; the 6th Geoscience and Environmental Management symposium; the 6th International Conference on Bioinformatics, Biotechnology, and Biomedical Engineering and the 6th International Conference on Smart and Innovative Agriculture.

The accepted and presented papers will be submitted for possible inclusion to proceedings that are indexed by Scopus/CPCI/DOAJ.
